Powder Skiing
Powder

One can never be
bored by powder skiing
because it is a special
gift of the relationship
between the earth and sky.

It comes in sufficient
amounts, in particular places
at certain times on this
earth. It lasts only a
limited amount of time
before the sun or wind changes it.
People devote their lives to it

"For the pleasure of being so purely
played by gravity and snow" -Dolores LaChapolle
