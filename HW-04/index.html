Somewhat Secret1 Thai Peanut Sauce

Ingredients (Where to Buy)


  • Brown Sugar
    • 1/4 cup
  • White Vinegar
    • 1/4 cup
  • Water
    • 1/2 cup
  • Red Chili Garlic Paste
    • 4 tbsp.
  • Peanut Butter
    • 1/3 cup
  • Soy Sauce
    • 7-8 splashes

Instructions


  1. Pick a container that is sealable and can contain all combined ingredients
  2. Combine the vinegar, brown sugar, red chili garlic paste, and Peanut Butter
  3. Shake vigorously until it is well blended.
  4. Put the soy sauce and water into container.
  5. Shake vigorously until all ingredients are blended or until your arms are tired.
  6. ENJOY!


We usually enjoy this sauce on a nice fresh salad or as the base sauce to the pizza.
