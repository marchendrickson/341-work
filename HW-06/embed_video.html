Safety First!!



When skiing in the backcountry it is extremely important that you have knowledge of the snowpack and other dangers associated with skiing. Avalanches are a very real concern wherever you may ski in the backcountry. With some classes and a mentor you may mitigate these risks that are inherent to backcountry travel.

This video is just a starting point that you may use to get to know the dangers and freedom that exist with backcountry skiing
