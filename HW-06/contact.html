Lolo Pass



Lolo pass is one of the easiest backcountry skiing spots to access in the Missoula Area. There are multiple places to park and ski. The basic approach to skiing at this pass is to park next to a bunch of cars and then follow the trail thaty has already been created by the manhy other people you will see that day.
