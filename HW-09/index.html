Planning your next big adventure can be a humongous task. Where do you start? How many miles a day can you travel? Is the route that you want to travel over private land? We want you to succeed at your next adventure!

Mapping routes is one of the fundamental things we do, however we would love to plan more for you. We can coordinate food and water drops at key points along any route that you choose. Another key aspect with longer trips is transportation, we can order plane tickets or give your friends a GPS coordinate where to pick you up or see if there is an UBER driver that can pick you up.

About: Marc Hendrickson has been climbing, skiing, and exploring the United States for 15 years. He started exploring in New England, climbing and skiing all over New Hampshire. Then moved out west and has not stopped his exploration and has traveled climbing extensively in the Southwest as well as Montana. He has done numerous backpacks throughout the western US and favorites include the Belly River in Glacier Natl. Park and the Dolores River in Colorado.
